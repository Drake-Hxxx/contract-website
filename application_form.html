Probationary Period and Regular Employment
The probationary period is five (5) working days, during which you will undergo relevant training and work task evaluations.
Upon passing the probationary period evaluation, you will become a regular employee of Publicis Groupe.
As a regular employee, you will be entitled to the following company benefits:

  • Health insurance
  • 401(k) retirement plan
  • Paid leave
  • Employee training and development opportunities
  • Remote work support equipment subsidy (subject to position)
